Adobe AI Extensions

Aide for Adobe CC

CEP panels that live inside Adobe Illustrator and InDesign and act as your AI scripting assistant and script manager. Describe what you want in plain English — Aide generates the ExtendScript code, previews it, and lets you execute it with one click. Save your scripts and export them to share with others, load local scripts, star your favorite scripts, and easily run them. Works with local AI models (via Ollama), or cloud providers like Google Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic, OpenRouter, and any OpenAI-compatible endpoint.
